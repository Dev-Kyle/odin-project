Alfredo Description

Alfredo is good, but what is Alfredo? In this recipe blog we'll be covering what truly comprises a Alfredo. Is Alfredo a pasta? Is pasta a Alfredo? Or even better, is a hotdog a sandwich? I hope you enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Plate
  • Fork
  • Alfredo

Steps

  1. Put plate on table
  2. Using fork, place Alfredo on plate
  3. Enjoy!
